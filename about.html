The person behind the lens

I'm Vik — a London-based photographer and videographer specialising in professional services, charities, corporate events and food & hospitality.

Before going freelance, I spent several years working in-house, producing content across a range of industries — including the automotive sector with Topaz Detailing, and financial services with Sedulo, a Top 100 UK professional services firm.

I work calmly and efficiently, fitting around you and your team rather than the other way around. No outsourcing, no layers — just a reliable partner who understands your brand.
